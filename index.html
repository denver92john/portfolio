Here's a little bit about me

I’m en route from graduating Bloc.io’s Full-Stack Web Developer bootcamp. I’ve always had an interest in coding, but never really tried to learn how to code. I’ve accumulated a diverse range of work experience over the past few years, but I haven’t really built upon my prior experiences to develop a career path for myself.

Coding has become a passion of mine and continues to reward my curiosity by exploring better solutions to any given problem. The satisfaction and pride gained in seeing the result of a final product, that works as intended, is comparable to driving by a thriving establishment and knowing that you contributed to its construction.

When I’m not trying to better myself as a coder, I’m trying to be outdoors. Whether it be kayaking, hiking, etc. No matter what I’m doing though, I’m usually listening to different podcasts, which is another major interest of mine. When I become a proficient full-stack developer, I’d like to develop myself in the podcasting industry, whether behind the scenes or on the mic.
